Venues &
Getting There

Two clubs host the Belgium tournament — KHC Dragons in Brasschaat, just north of Antwerp, and HC Olympia on the south side of the city. Coming from the Netherlands? It's an easy hop: direct trains run from Rotterdam to Antwerpen-Centraal all day.

Main venue · Opening Ceremony

KHC Dragons

Sportendreef 1
2930 Brasschaat, Belgium

  • 🏑 3 water-based pitches + 1 semi-sand pitch
  • 🎉 Opening Ceremony · August 6, 7:00 PM
  • 🌳 In Brasschaat's park belt, ~30–40 min north of central Antwerp by car or bus
  • 🚗 Outside Antwerp's low-emission zone — easy to drive to

Belgium travel basics

Coming from NL?

  • 🚆 Direct trains Rotterdam Centraal → Antwerpen-Centraal: ~30 min on the high-speed line, ~1 hr by intercity
  • 🚗 Driving from Rotterdam: ~1 hr 15 via the A16/E19 — Brasschaat sits just off the E19 north of Antwerp
  • ⚠️ Central Antwerp (inside the Ring) is a low-emission zone — register a foreign-plated car in advance at lez / slimnaarantwerpen.be
  • 🚌 Local buses & route planning: Smart Ways to Antwerp (the host's recommended planner)
