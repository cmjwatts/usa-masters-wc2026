Venue &
Getting There

One club hosts the whole O65+ tournament: BHV Push in Breda — a classic Dutch hockey club an easy ride from Breda Centraal, and only a short train hop from Rotterdam or the Belgium venues.

Travel basics

Getting to Breda

  • 🚆 Direct intercity Rotterdam Centraal → Breda: ~25 min
  • 🚆 From Antwerp (Belgium tournament): direct trains, ~35–45 min
  • 🚗 Driving from Rotterdam: ~45 min via the A16
  • 🇧🇪 Watching family at both August tournaments? Breda ↔ Brasschaat is ~50 min by car
